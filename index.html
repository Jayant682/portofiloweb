Tim Berners-Lee

I am the founder of World Wide Web

Hi!

Sir Tim Berners-Lee invented the World Wide Web in 1989, Sir Tim is the Director of World Wide Web Consortium (W3C), a Web standards organization found in 1994 which devolopes interoperable technoligies (specifications, guidelines, software, and tools) to lead the web to its full potential. He is a Director of the World Wide Web Foundation which is launched in 2009 to coordinate efforts to further the potential of the Web to benefit humanity.

In 2001 he became a Fellow of the Royal Society. He has been the recipent of several international awards including the Japan Prize, the Prince of Asturias Foundation Prize, the Millennium Technology Prize ,and Germany's Die Quadriga award. In 2004 he was knighted by H.M.Queen Elizabath amd in 2007 he was awarded the Order of Merit. In 2009 he was elected a forign associate of the National Acadamy of Sciences.
